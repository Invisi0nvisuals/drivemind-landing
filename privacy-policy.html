📄 Privacy Policy for DriveMind

Effective Date: July 11, 2025
Last Updated: July 11, 2025

Welcome to DriveMind! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services — including the DriveMind app, website (https://drivemind.app), and chatbot assistant.

🔍 1. What We Collect

We collect only the information we need to help you:

Data TypeWhy We Collect It
NameSo we know who you are
Email addressTo send updates, diagnostics, or support replies
Vehicle infoTo help you understand warning lights, codes, and TSBs
Chat interactionsTo improve app performance and provide relevant answers
Device dataBasic info like OS type and browser (for security/debugging)

We don’t collect sensitive information like credit card numbers or geolocation unless you opt in.

📲 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your info to:

  • Help you interpret vehicle issues
  • Send app-related updates or replies
  • Improve the chatbot and user experience
  • Build new features responsibly

🛡️ 3. How We Protect Your Data

We use modern encryption, HTTPS security, and limit internal access to your data. Your information will never be sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing.

🇪🇺 4. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you’re in the European Union, you have these rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to Rectify: Fix any errors in your data
  • Right to Erase: Delete your data at any time
  • Right to Restrict/Object: Limit how we process your data
  • Right to Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, email us at: support@drivemind.app

We process your data based on:

  • Your consent (when signing up or chatting)
  • Our legitimate interest to run the app
  • Legal compliance (e.g. debugging or fraud prevention)

🌎 5. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data may be stored securely in the United States and/or in EU-compliant cloud providers. We work only with trusted services like Aidbase, Supabase, or Google Cloud.

📬 6. Communications

If you join the waitlist, submit a ticket, or engage with our chatbot, you may receive:

  • Confirmation emails
  • Important updates
  • Opt-in product announcements

You can unsubscribe at any time.

🧼 7. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as needed to provide service, or until you ask us to delete it.

🔗 8. Third-Party Tools

We may use third-party platforms like:

  • Aidbase – for live chat and support tickets
  • Google Analytics – for anonymized usage stats
  • Mailgun / Brevo – for emails or waitlist management

These services are GDPR-aligned and follow strict data practices.

📘 9. Cookies

We use essential and performance cookies to make the website work better. No creepy tracking. You’ll be notified and can opt out where applicable.

📝 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes. If so, we’ll update the "Last Updated" date and notify users as needed.
