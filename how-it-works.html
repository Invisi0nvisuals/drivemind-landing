How DriveMind Works

From dashboard panic to instant clarity — here's how we empower drivers.

Just like AutoZone’s Fix Finder, we start the moment a light appears on your dash.

Step 2: Open DriveMind

No hardware, no app store download. Just scan the light with your phone.

Step 3: Get a plain-English report

Understand what’s wrong, what it costs, and whether it’s urgent.
